[h1]Jese和Beta的伤害计算[/h1]

[b]灵感来源[/b]：  
[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2986919931]Better Damage Calc 更好的伤害计算[/url] | [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2827999994]DPSExtreme[/url]  

在BOSS战结束后，这个Mod会自动计算并展示以下数据：  

*伤害源排名：统计所有玩家的伤害来源（包括武器，召唤物，射弹等）的伤害贡献排名。  
*玩家伤害排名：统计所有玩家的总伤害贡献排名。  
*战斗时长：你/你们在这场BOSS战中跟BOSS打了多久。  
*平均每秒伤害：这场战斗中你/你们每秒对BOSS造成的伤害的平均值。
*受击次数：你/你们在这场BOSS战中挨打了多少次，包括被免疫或闪避的伤害也会被算进其中。

[b]支持本地[/b] ✅
[b]支持多人[/b] ✅

无需进行其他配置，自动运行。

[h1]Jese&Beta's Damage Calc[/h1]

Inspiration Sources:  
[url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2986919931]Better Damage Calc[/url] | [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2827999994]DPSExtreme[/url]  

After a boss fight, this mod will automatically calculate and display the following data:  


*Damage Source Ranking: Tracks the damage contribution of all damage sources (including weapons, minions, projectiles, etc.) used by players.  
*Player Damage Ranking: Ranks the total damage contribution of all players.  
*Fight Duration: How long you/your team fought the boss.  
*Average DPS: The average damage dealt per second by you/your team during the fight.  
*Hit Taken Count: How many times you/your team has been hit by the boss during the fight, including immune or evaded damage.  

[b]Supports Single Player[/b] ✅  
[b]Supports Multiplayer[/b] ✅  

No additional configuration required—runs automatically!
